How to set SMART goals

  • S- Specific: What, Where, and Why?
  • M- Measurable: How will you track progress?
  • A- Achievable: Be Realistic. What steps will you take?
  • R- Relevant: What will this goal help you acheive?
  • T- Timely: Give your goal a specific date!
